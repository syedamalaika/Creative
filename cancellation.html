Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Subscription Services

Monthly recurring services such as SEO or Ad Management require a 30-day written notice for cancellation to ensure a smooth transition and handoff of assets.

2. Project-Based Work

If you choose to cancel a project before completion, you will be billed only for the work completed up to the date of cancellation.

3. How to Cancel

To initiate a cancellation, please email your dedicated account manager or contact us at Hello@creative360pro.com.
